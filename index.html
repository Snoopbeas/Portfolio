jupiter

you look down at your radar. you've reached jupiter, a gas giant. but instead of gasses, it is filled with flowers and trees and alien wildlife. you pause, staring with awe at the new world around you. a world you never imagined could exist. the grass is thick and lavish with tiny creatures. the petals are dripping with gold. insects whizz and fancy in the beating sunlight. you take a breath of sweetest air.
