About

WWhile this is a animated kids film, it's contents and story almost reminds me of psychedelic films because of some scenes and themes. Many others who also watched this movie would describe it as a 'acid trip' in funnier terms, which I understood when watching it for the first time.

I wanted to take the seemingly cute aesthetic this movie and give it a almost analog horror twist because of its oddly serious themes in the movie that take it in a silly and unserious tone.
